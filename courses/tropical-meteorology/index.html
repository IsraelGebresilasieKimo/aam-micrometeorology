Tropical Meteorology

This course focuses on atmospheric processes in the tropics, including tropical circulation systems, the Intertropical Convergence Zone (ITCZ), monsoons, tropical waves, convection, tropical cyclones, air–sea interaction, and climate variability in tropical regions.
