Aviation Meteorology

This course focuses on meteorological conditions affecting aviation safety and operations. It includes study of wind shear, turbulence, visibility, fog, cloud types, thunderstorms, icing conditions, aviation weather observation systems, METAR/TAF interpretation, and flight weather forecasting for safe air navigation.
