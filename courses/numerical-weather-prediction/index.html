Numerical Weather Prediction

This course introduces the principles and applications of Numerical Weather Prediction (NWP), including atmospheric governing equations, numerical discretization techniques, model initialization, data assimilation, forecast verification, and operational forecasting systems. Students gain an understanding of how modern weather prediction models simulate atmospheric processes and support weather forecasting and decision-making.
