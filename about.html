About Me

I hold a BSc in Meteorology and Hydrology and an MSc in Climate Change and Development. I currently serve as a lecturer and researcher at the Faculty of Meteorology and Hydrology, Arba Minch University, Ethiopia.

My academic interests include atmospheric boundary-layer processes, urban wind dynamics, turbulence, and their implications for weather-robust and safe Advanced Air Mobility operations.

I am preparing for PhD applications in topics related to micrometeorology, urban CFD, and AAM.
